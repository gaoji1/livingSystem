How to start your liveï¼š

To start live,you must install a software to push your live stream to the server. That could be Adobe Flash Media Live Encoderor OBS Studioor other software of the same type.After installing pushing software,follow the steps below.

1.Make sure your pushing software can capture your video,if you need to capture video from your camera,don't forget to open it from your operation system.

2.Set up your software.Fill rtmp://localhost/myApp in URL option.Fill your streamname in StreamName option.If you forget your streamname,you can find it in user manage page.

3.Click Push Button of your software and click the Start button below.If prompt live Start, then congratulations,you have started up your live broadcast!
